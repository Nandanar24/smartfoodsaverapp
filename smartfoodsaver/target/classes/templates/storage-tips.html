Storage Tips

Food lasts longer when stored properly, and SmartFoodSaver gives you easy, practical tips to help.

Learn how to: Keep fruit and vegetables fresh. Store dairy products safely. Extend the life of meat, bread, and pantry staples.

  • Carrots last longer when stored in a sealed container in the fridge.
  • Keep potatoes in a cool, dark place to prevent sprouting.

Small adjustments like these can make a big difference in how long your food stays fresh.
